
# Angel Dash — Faith-Tech Investor Directory

A clean, filterable investor dashboard built for pre-seed and seed-stage founders building in the faith-tech space.



![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-green)




![Built With](https://img.shields.io/badge/built%20with-React-blue)



## What's Inside

- **14 curated investors** — funds, angel networks, accelerators, and VCs with proven faith-tech portfolios
- **Fit scoring** — each investor scored 1–10 for alignment with AI-powered faith consumer apps
- **Filter by tag** — TOP MATCH, STRONG FIT, APPLY NOW, WARM OUTREACH, FUTURE TARGET, REGIONAL LEAD
- **CSV export** — one click copies a spreadsheet-ready investor list to your clipboard
- **Expandable rows** — click any investor to see full thesis notes, check size, stage, and website

## Who This Is For

Founders building in:
- Faith-based AI / consumer apps
- Christian community platforms
- Prayer, devotional, or scripture apps
- Church tech and ministry software
